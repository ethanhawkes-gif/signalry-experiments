FAQ

Common questions

How is this different from Apollo or Clay?

Apollo and Clay give you data and tools — you still have to manage sequences, review emails, and stay on top of the pipeline. Pitchflow does the entire job. You don't touch it; you just get the notification when someone's interested.

Who writes the emails?

Our AI, trained on thousands of high-performing cold emails. Every message is personalized to the specific prospect — their company context, recent signals, and your product's fit. Not mail-merge. Genuinely personalized.

Do I have to give you access to my email?

No. Pitchflow sends from a dedicated domain and sending address we set up for you. Your personal or work inbox stays clean. Replies route back through our system so we can notify you instantly.

What if I want to review emails before they go out?

We'll set up an approval gate in onboarding if you want it. Most customers let it run fully autonomously after reviewing the first batch — the quality holds up.
